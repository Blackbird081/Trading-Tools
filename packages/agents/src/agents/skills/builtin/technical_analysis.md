# Technical Analysis Process

When asked to technically analyze a stock, do it in the following order:

## Step 1: Collect Data
- Get OHLCV data for at least the last 200 sessions
- Check the average trading volume of 20 sessions

## Step 2: Trend Analysis
- **MA50 vs MA200**: Golden cross (MA50 > MA200) = long-term uptrend
- **Price vs MA20**: Price above MA20 = positive short-term trend
- **Price Channel**: Identify important support/resistance

## Step 3: Momentum Indicators
- **RSI (14)**: 
- < 30: Oversold → potential buy signal
- > 70: Overbought → potential sell signal
- 30-70: Neutral
- **MACD (12,26,9)**:
- Bullish cross (MACD > Signal): buy signal
- Bearish cross (MACD < Signal): sell signal
- Positive/negative histogram: confirms momentum

## Step 4: Volatility
- **Bollinger Bands (20,2)**:
- Price at lower band: oversold, can bounce
- Price at upper band: overbought, possible pullback
- Band squeeze: prepare for breakout

## Step 5: Volume Confirmation
- Volume spike (> 2x average): confirms the signal
- Low volume when price increases: weak signal, need to be cautious

## Step 6: Summarize Scores
Calculate composite score (-10 to +10):
- RSI oversold: +3, RSI overbought: -3
- MACD bullish cross: +3, bearish cross: -3
- BB below lower: +2, above upper: -2
- Golden cross: +2, Death cross: -2

## Conclude
- Score ≥ 5: BUY
- Score ≤ -5: SELL
- -5 < Score < 5: NEUTRAL

**Note on the Vietnamese market**: Ceiling/floor price ±7% (HOSE) may limit momentum. Check if the price is approaching the ceiling/floor.
